# Presentation Skills

* Focus on delivering the information not caring about your image.

* Must haves:
    - Interactivity
    - Video (< 2min) with explanation
    - Instead of questions? Ask for any comments or additions
    - Examples, numbers in most slides -> make it relative to the audience
    - Summary
    - Transitions
    - Let audience be engaged in a positive and light way
    - Practice a lot on record

- Body Posture:
    - Eye contact (radar)
    - Facial expressions
    - Hand gestures
    - Minimal movement
    - Give audience your face, not side
    - Stand 11, free your hands
    - Fix clothes once before start, never focus to it again -> attention of audience is on your hands

- Voice:
    - Non monotonic -> say same sentence with different tones
    - Clear, loud voice
    - Good pace
    - No fillers -> pause

- Content
    - Relative to audience
    - Logical, seamless flow
    - Know what you present
    - Easy to folllow
    - Examples
    - Practical connection to topic
    - Use infographics, charts, images, videos
